ToYouth

ToYouth is an Indonesian music duo made up of Semmy and Uki — two artists from different cities who found the perfect creative spark together.

Semmy lives in Bali. Uki lives in Bekasi. Separated by distance but connected by their shared passion for music, they started collaborating remotely — sending voice notes, hopping on late-night calls, and turning simple ideas into full songs from their bedrooms.

What began as a one-time project quickly grew into something more. Now, ToYouth is a duo with a unique sound, blending pop, indie, and progressive elements into music that feels personal, vibrant, and honest.

Semmy is the lead singer and songwriter, known for his strong sense of melody and lyrics that come from real emotions and moments.
Uki is the guitarist, producer, and co-lead vocalist, adding texture and energy through his guitar work and production skills.

Together, they keep pushing themselves — learning, experimenting, and always evolving. Distance, time, even creative blocks never stop them. If anything, those challenges only fuel their drive.

Two cities. One sound. Always forward.
